<p><p>The airway catheter market comprises two primary types: disposable airway catheters and reusable airway catheters. Disposable airway catheters are designed for single-use, ensuring sterility and reducing the risk of infection, making them ideal for emergency settings and procedures. In contrast, reusable airway catheters are crafted for multiple uses, typically featuring materials that can withstand sterilization processes.
