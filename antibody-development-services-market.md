<p><strong>The Antibody Development Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monoclonal Antibody Development</li><li>Polyclonal Antibody Development</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Antibody Development Services market encompasses two primary types: monoclonal and polyclonal antibody development. Monoclonal antibody development involves creating identical copies of a single type of immune cell, resulting in antibodies with high specificity for targeted antigens, widely used in diagnostics and therapeutics. In contrast, polyclonal antibody development generates a heterogeneous mixture of antibodies from different immune cells, providing a broader response to multiple antigens. Both methods are crucial in research, clinical applications, and therapeutic interventions across various diseases.</p></p>
