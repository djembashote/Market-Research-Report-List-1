<p><strong>The Asphalt Recycling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Recycling</li><li>Cold Recycling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Asphalt recycling involves two primary types: hot recycling and cold recycling. Hot recycling utilizes reclaimed asphalt pavement (RAP) that is heated and combined with new materials, enhancing flexibility and performance, making it ideal for high-stress applications. In contrast, cold recycling processes RAP without heating, often using emulsions or foamed asphalt, making it cost-effective and environmentally friendly, suitable for lower traffic areas. Both methods promote sustainability by reducing waste and conserving resources in road construction and maintenance.</p></p>
