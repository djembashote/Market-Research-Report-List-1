<p><ul><li>Gravimetric Automated Compounding System</li><li>Volumetric Automated Compounding System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automated Compounding System (ACS) market is divided into two primary types: gravimetric and volumetric systems. Gravimetric Automated Compounding Systems utilize weight measurements to ensure precise ingredient ratios, minimizing errors and enhancing accuracy in compounding processes. In contrast, Volumetric Automated Compounding Systems rely on volume measurements, allowing for quick and efficient mixing of components. Both systems are essential in pharmaceutical and medical applications, improving compounding reliability, reducing manual labor, and enhancing patient safety through consistent medication preparation.</p></p>
