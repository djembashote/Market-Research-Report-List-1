<p><ul><li>Horizontal End Side-Load</li><li>Top-Load</li><li>Wraparound</li><li>Vertical Leaflet</li><li>Vertical Sleeve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Beverage Carton Packaging Machinery market includes several types tailored for different packaging needs. Horizontal End Side-Load machines efficiently load cartons from the side, ideal for high-speed production. Top-Load packaging facilitates top entry for easy carton placement. Wraparound machines provide versatile packaging by wrapping material around products, while Vertical Leaflet systems insert leaflets into cartons vertically. Lastly, Vertical Sleeve machines apply a sleeve of material around items, ensuring secure packaging. Each type serves unique operational requirements in the beverage industry.</p></p>
