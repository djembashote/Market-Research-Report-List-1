<p><p>The automotive CO2 heat pump air conditioning market is divided into two types: direct and indirect. Direct market systems utilize CO2 as a refrigerant, directly converting CO2 in a closed loop for heating and cooling, enhancing efficiency and reducing greenhouse gas emissions. Indirect market systems, on the other hand, use CO2 in conjunction with traditional refrigerants, providing thermal management by transferring heat without a direct CO2 flow. Both approaches aim to improve energy efficiency and sustainability in vehicle climate control.</p></p>
