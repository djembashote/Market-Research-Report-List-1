In contrast, Continuous Type Blood Component Extractors facilitate a steady flow of blood component separation, allowing for real-time processing and higher efficiency, ideal for larger blood banks or hospitals with consistent volume requirements. Both types cater to different operational needs in blood management.</p></p>
