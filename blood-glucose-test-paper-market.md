<p><strong>The Blood Glucose Test Paper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glucose Dehydrogenase</li><li>Glucose Oxidase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood glucose test paper market is primarily categorized into two types based on the enzymatic reactions used: Glucose Dehydrogenase and Glucose Oxidase. Glucose Dehydrogenase test strips utilize an enzyme that catalyzes the oxidation of glucose, providing high specificity and reduced interference from other substances. In contrast, Glucose Oxidase strips rely on the oxidation of glucose to hydrogen peroxide, which can be affected by various compounds. Each type has unique advantages, influencing their application in diabetic management and monitoring.</p></p>
