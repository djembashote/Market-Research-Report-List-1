<p><p>The CaF2 lens market is categorized into two main types: spherical lenses and aspheric lenses. Spherical lenses have a uniform curvature, providing consistent optical performance for various applications such as cameras and microscopes. In contrast, aspheric lenses feature a non-uniform surface profile, which minimizes optical aberrations and enhances image quality, making them ideal for high-performance optical systems. Both types are valued for their ability to transmit light efficiently and are crucial in industries like telecommunications, medical imaging, and scientific research.</p></p>
