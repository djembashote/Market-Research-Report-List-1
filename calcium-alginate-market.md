<p><p>The Calcium Alginate market is segmented into High M (Mannuronic acid) and High G (Guluronic acid) types based on their molecular composition. High M Calcium Alginates are rich in mannuronic acid, offering flexibility and soft gel formation, ideal for applications requiring elasticity, such as wound care and textiles. High G Calcium Alginates, with higher guluronic acid content, form firmer, more rigid gels, making them suitable for applications needing structural integrity, like food stabilization and pharmaceuticals. Both types cater to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
