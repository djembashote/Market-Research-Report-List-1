<p><p>The car chassis dynamometer market features two primary types: single roller and multi-roller dynos. Single roller chassis dynos are simpler and compact, using one roller to efficiently test a variety of vehicles, making them ideal for smaller operations. In contrast, multi-roller chassis dynos consist of multiple rollers that accommodate more complex testing scenarios, providing enhanced accuracy and the ability to simulate real-world driving conditions for a range of high-performance vehicles. Both types serve distinct needs within automotive performance evaluation.</p></p>
