<p><p>Cellular V2X (Vehicle-to-Everything) communication encompasses three primary market types. Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V) communication enables cars to exchange information directly, enhancing safety and reducing accidents. Vehicle-to-Infrastructure (V2I) communication connects vehicles to road infrastructure, improving traffic management and optimizing routes. Vehicle-to-Pedestrian (V2P) communication allows vehicles to communicate with pedestrians' devices, enhancing safety by alerting drivers to nearby pedestrians. These technologies collectively aim to create safer, more efficient transportation systems, fostering advancements in autonomous driving and smart city initiatives.</p></p>
