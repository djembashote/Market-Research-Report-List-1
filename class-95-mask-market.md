<p><strong>The Class 95 Mask Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>N95</li><li>P95</li><li>R95</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Class 95 masks include N95, P95, and R95 categories, each defined by their filtration efficiency and resistance to particular substances. N95 masks filter out at least 95% of airborne particles but are not resistant to oil. P95 masks also filter 95% of particles and are designed to be oil-resistant, making them suitable for more challenging environments. R95 masks filter 95% of particles but have limited oil resistance, making them appropriate for some oil-based environments but not suitable for extended use with oil exposure.</p></p>
