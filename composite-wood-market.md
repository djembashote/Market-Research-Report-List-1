<p><strong>The Composite Wood Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plywood</li><li>Oriented strand board (OSB)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The composite wood market primarily consists of plywood and oriented strand board (OSB). Plywood is made of thin layers of wood veneer glued together, providing strength and versatility for various applications, including furniture and construction. OSB, on the other hand, is manufactured by compressing wood strands and adhesives, offering excellent structural performance and cost-effectiveness, making it popular in residential and commercial building. Both products are eco-friendly alternatives to solid wood, catering to increasing demand for sustainable materials.</p></p>
