<p><p>The Controlled Descent System market is categorized into two primary types: manual operation and automatic operation. Manual operation systems require human intervention for descent control, allowing users to adjust their descent based on environmental conditions or personal preference. In contrast, automatic operation systems utilize advanced technology to autonomously manage descent, providing precision and reliability without direct user input. Each type caters to different user needs, with manual systems often favored for their hands-on approach and automatic systems preferred for convenience and safety.</p></p>
