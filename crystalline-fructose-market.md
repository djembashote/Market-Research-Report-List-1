<p><strong>The Crystalline Fructose Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Starch Hydrolysis</li><li>Sucrose Hydrolysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Crystalline Fructose market can be categorized into two main types based on production methods: Starch Hydrolysis and Sucrose Hydrolysis. Starch Hydrolysis involves the enzymatic breakdown of starch into glucose, which is then converted into fructose, resulting in high-fructose syrup. In contrast, Sucrose Hydrolysis involves the enzymatic splitting of sucrose into glucose and fructose, directly yielding crystalline fructose. Both methods cater to various applications in food and beverage industries, driven by the demand for sweeteners and healthier alternatives.</p></p>
