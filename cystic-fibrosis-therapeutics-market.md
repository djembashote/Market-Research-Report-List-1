<p><p>The cystic fibrosis therapeutics market consists of several key types aimed at managing the condition. CFTR modulators enhance the functioning of defective CFTR proteins. Mucolytics help dissolve thick mucus, improving airway clearance. Pancreatic enzyme replacement therapy (PERT) aids digestion by supplying necessary enzymes. Anti-infectives target respiratory infections common in cystic fibrosis patients, while bronchodilators relax airway muscles to facilitate breathing. Together, these therapeutics address the multifaceted challenges of cystic fibrosis and improve patients' quality of life.</p></p>
