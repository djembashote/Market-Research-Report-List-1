<p><p>Digital Business Support Systems (DBSS) are categorized into two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions require installation and maintenance on local servers, providing businesses with greater control and customization but often involving higher upfront costs and IT resource commitments. Conversely, cloud-based systems operate on remote servers, offering scalability, flexibility, and lower initial investments. They enable users to access services from anywhere with internet connectivity, promoting seamless collaboration and rapid deployment while reducing the need for extensive IT infrastructure.</p></p>
