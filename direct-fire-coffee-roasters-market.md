<p><strong>The Direct-Fire Coffee Roasters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Drum Roasters</li><li>Perforated Drum Roasters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct-fire coffee roasters primarily fall into two market types: solid drum roasters and perforated drum roasters. Solid drum roasters use a continuous solid drum to roast coffee beans, providing a consistent heat distribution and flavor profile. In contrast, perforated drum roasters allow for higher airflow and quicker roasting times due to their perforated design, promoting a more even roast and enhanced control over temperature. Both types cater to different roasting styles and preferences in the coffee industry, appealing to various customers and needs.</p></p>
