<p><p>The Direct-Push Equipment market comprises various types, primarily categorized into hydraulic, vibration, and others. Hydraulic systems utilize pressurized fluid to drive the equipment, offering precise control and efficiency in subsurface sampling. Vibration techniques rely on mechanical oscillations to penetrate soil, enhancing sampling speed and accuracy. Together, these technologies are essential for environmental assessments, geotechnical investigations, and site characterization.</p></p>
