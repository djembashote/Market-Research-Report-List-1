<p><ul><li>"BMT Driven Turning Holders"</li><li>"VDI Driven Turning Holders"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Driven turning holders are specialized cutting tools used in machining processes, categorized mainly into two types: BMT (Boring-Milling-Tool) driven turning holders and VDI (Verein Deutscher Ingenieure) driven turning holders. BMT driven turning holders offer a modular design, providing flexibility and ease of tool changes, making them ideal for complex machining tasks. VDI driven turning holders, on the other hand, are known for their precision and rigidity, commonly used in CNC lathes for high-speed operations and improved surface finishes.</p></p>
