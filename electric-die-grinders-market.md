<p><p>Electric die grinders are versatile tools primarily categorized into angle and straight grinders. Angle grinders feature a rotating head set at a right angle, allowing for efficient grinding and cutting in tight spaces. They are often used in metalworking for weld smoothing or surface preparation. Straight grinders, on the other hand, have a collet that accepts various attachments, offering precision in tight areas and intricate details. Both types are essential in industries like automotive, construction, and metal fabrication for precision tasks.</p></p>
