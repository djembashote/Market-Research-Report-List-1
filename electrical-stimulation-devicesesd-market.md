Vagus Nerve Stimulation (VNS) devices manage epilepsy and depression by stimulating the vagus nerve. Sacral Nerve Stimulation (SNS) targets pelvic floor issues, while Gastric Electrical Stimulation (GES) aids in obesity treatment. Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation (TENS) provides pain relief through skin stimulation, and Neuromuscular Electrical Stimulation (NMES/EMS) enhances muscle function. Other devices cater to specific therapeutic needs.</p></p>
