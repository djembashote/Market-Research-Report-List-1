<p><p>The Elevator Overspeed Governor market is divided into two primary types: electronic and mechanical. Electronic governors utilize advanced sensors and microprocessors to monitor elevator speeds with high accuracy, offering faster response times and enhanced safety features. In contrast, mechanical governors rely on traditional mechanical systems, including centrifugal weights and springs, to detect overspeed conditions. While electronic types are gaining popularity due to their precision and reliability, mechanical types continue to be favored for their simplicity and established performance in various elevator applications.</p></p>
