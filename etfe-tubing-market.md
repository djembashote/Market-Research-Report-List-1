<p><strong>The ETFE Tubing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heat Shrinkable Tubing</li><li>Extruded Tubing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ETFE tubing market comprises two primary types: heat shrinkable tubing and extruded tubing. Heat shrinkable tubing is designed to shrink upon heating, providing a tight fit and insulation for wires and components. It is ideal for applications requiring secure seals and protection against environmental factors. Extruded tubing, on the other hand, is produced through a continuous process, resulting in uniform thickness and flexibility, making it suitable for a wide range of industrial and commercial applications, including electrical insulation and fluid transfer.</p></p>
