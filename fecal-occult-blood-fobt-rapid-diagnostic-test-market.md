<p><strong>The Fecal Occult Blood (FOBT) Rapid Diagnostic Test Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Immunochemical Fecal Occult Blood Test (iFOBT or FIT)</li><li>Guaiac Fecal Occult Blood Test (gFOBT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fecal Occult Blood (FOBT) Rapid Diagnostic Test market includes two main types: the Immunochemical Fecal Occult Blood Test (iFOBT or FIT) and the Guaiac Fecal Occult Blood Test (gFOBT). iFOBT detects human hemoglobin using antibodies, offering higher specificity and sensitivity for colorectal cancer screening. In contrast, gFOBT relies on chemical reactions to identify blood in stool samples, but it may produce false positives from dietary sources. Both tests are crucial for early cancer detection and screening programs.</p></p>
