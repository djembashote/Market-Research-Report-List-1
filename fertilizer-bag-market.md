<p><p>The fertilizer bag market comprises various materials catering to diverse agricultural needs. Low-density polyethylene (LDPE) bags are lightweight, moisture-resistant, and flexible, making them ideal for smaller quantities. Polypropylene (PP) bags are durable and can hold heavier loads, offering excellent strength and resistance to chemicals. Burlap bags are eco-friendly, breathable, and biodegradable, suitable for organic fertilizers. Other materials may include paper or specialized woven fabrics, providing alternatives based on cost, environmental impact, and specific storage requirements for fertilizers.</p></p>
