<p><strong>The Fiber Bragg Grating Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Evenly</li><li>Inhomogeneous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber Bragg Grating (FBG) devices are categorized into two market types: evenly distributed and inhomogeneous. The evenly distributed market features FBGs with uniform grating periods, ideal for applications requiring consistent wavelength reflection across the entire fiber length, such as sensing and telecommunications. In contrast, the inhomogeneous market includes FBGs with varying grating periods, allowing for selective wavelength filtering and enhanced functionality in specialized applications. These distinctions influence the devices' performance and suitability for different industrial needs.</p></p>
