<p><p>The Fluidized Bed Cooler market is primarily categorized into two types: Vibrating Fluidized Bed Coolers and Static Fluidized Bed Coolers. Vibrating Fluidized Bed Coolers employ mechanical vibrations to enhance the fluidization process, improving heat transfer efficiency and enabling better control over particle movement. In contrast, Static Fluidized Bed Coolers rely on consistent airflow to maintain fluidization without mechanical agitation, making them suitable for different applications. Both types are utilized in various industries for cooling granular materials effectively.</p></p>
