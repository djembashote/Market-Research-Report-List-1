<p><p>The food automatic tray sealer market comprises two main types: semi-automatic and fully automatic tray sealers. Semi-automatic food tray sealers require manual intervention for loading and unloading trays but automate the sealing process, making them suitable for small to medium-scale operations. Fully automatic food tray sealers, on the other hand, offer a complete automated solution, managing the entire sealing process, including tray handling, for high-volume production, enhancing efficiency and reducing labor costs for large-scale manufacturers.</p></p>
