<p><strong>The Full Bridge Driver Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>With PWM</li><li>Without PWM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Full Bridge Driver market can be categorized into two main types based on the incorporation of Pulse Width Modulation (PWM). </p><p>1. **With PWM**: This type utilizes PWM signals to control the output voltage and current supplied to the motor, allowing for efficient speed and torque control in various applications, including robotics and electric vehicles.</p><p>2. **Without PWM**: This type operates without PWM signals, delivering fixed voltage levels, typically used in simpler applications where precise control is not required, such as basic DC motor drives.</p></p>
