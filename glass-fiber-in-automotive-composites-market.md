<p><p>Glass fiber in automotive composites encompasses various types, each with unique properties and applications. Sheet Molding Compound (SMC) and Bulk Molding Compound (BMC) are versatile, thermoset materials ideal for complex shapes. Glass Mat Thermoplastic (GMT) and Short Fiber Thermoplastic (SFT) offer improved impact resistance, while Long Fiber Thermoplastic (LFT) and Continuous Fiber Thermoplastic (CFT) enhance structural integrity. Phenolic Molding Compound (PMC) provides excellent fire resistance, making it suitable for specific applications. Collectively, these composites enhance vehicle performance and durability.</p></p>
