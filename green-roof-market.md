<p><p>The green-roof market comprises three main types: extensive, semi-intensive, and intensive green roofs. Extensive green roofs feature shallow substrates and low-maintenance vegetation, ideal for urban areas to improve insulation and reduce stormwater runoff. Semi-intensive roofs combine elements of both extensive and intensive systems, allowing for a wider variety of plants with moderate maintenance needs. Intensive green roofs have deeper substrates, supporting diverse planting options, including trees and shrubs, and require more irrigation and maintenance, making them suited for recreational spaces.</p></p>
