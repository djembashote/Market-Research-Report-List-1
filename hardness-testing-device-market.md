<p><p>The hardness testing device market is primarily classified into three types: Vickers, Rockwell, and Brinell. Vickers tests measure hardness through a diamond pyramid indenter, suitable for various materials, providing precise results. Rockwell uses different scales based on depth of indentation, offering quick and user-friendly results for metals. Brinell, utilizing a hardened steel or carbide ball, is ideal for assessing larger samples and softer materials. Each method serves specific applications across industries, influencing material selection and quality control processes.</p></p>
