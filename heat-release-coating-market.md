<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based</li><li>Water-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heat release coating market comprises two primary types: solvent-based and water-based coatings. Solvent-based coatings utilize organic solvents to dissolve resins, offering high durability and excellent film formation, making them ideal for challenging environments. In contrast, water-based coatings use water as the primary solvent, providing environmental benefits and reduced VOC emissions. While water-based options are generally easier to apply and clean up, solvent-based coatings are preferred for their robust performance in extreme conditions. Both types cater to varying industrial and commercial needs.</p></p>
