<p><p>The heavy-duty chain hoist market comprises two main types: manual chain hoists and electric chain hoists. Manual chain hoists operate through human effort, using a hand-operated chain mechanism to lift heavy loads, making them suitable for limited or intermittent use. In contrast, electric chain hoists utilize electric motors to automate the lifting process, offering higher efficiency, speed, and ease of use for frequent and heavy lifting applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, enhancing material handling capabilities.</p></p>
