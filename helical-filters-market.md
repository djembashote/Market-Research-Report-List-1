<p><p>The helical filters market is categorized into three main types based on frequency ranges: VHF (Very High Frequency), UHF (Ultra High Frequency), and SHF (Super High Frequency). VHF filters operate between 30 MHz to 300 MHz, commonly used in television and radio broadcasting. UHF filters, spanning 300 MHz to 3 GHz, are utilized in mobile communication and satellite systems. SHF filters, covering 3 GHz to 30 GHz, find applications in radar and microwave communication, offering precise signal processing for various technologies.</p></p>
