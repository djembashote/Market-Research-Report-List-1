<p><p>High Efficiency Hetero-junction Solar Cells (HJT) can be classified into two main market types: Monofacial Cells and Bifacial Cells. Monofacial Cells capture sunlight from one side, making them suitable for conventional installations where space is limited. In contrast, Bifacial Cells harness sunlight from both sides, enhancing energy yield by utilizing reflected light from surfaces below. This dual-sided capability allows Bifacial Cells to be more efficient, especially in areas with high albedo, thus appealing to both residential and commercial markets.</p></p>
