<p><p>The high-pressure waterjet market features three main types: Impingement Jet, Continuous Jet, and Mixing Jet. Impingement Jet technology focuses on directing highly pressurized water to impact surfaces for cutting or cleaning. Continuous Jet maintains a steady flow of water, ideal for applications requiring consistent force. Mixing Jet combines water with abrasives for enhanced cutting capabilities in various materials. Each type serves unique industrial applications, from manufacturing to cleaning, showcasing the versatility and efficiency of high-pressure waterjet techniques.</p></p>
