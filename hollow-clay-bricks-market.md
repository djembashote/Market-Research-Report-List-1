<p><strong>The Hollow Clay Bricks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertically Perforated Clay Bricks</li><li>Horizontally Perforated Clay Bricks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hollow clay bricks market comprises two primary types: vertically perforated and horizontally perforated clay bricks. Vertically perforated clay bricks feature vertical holes that enhance thermal insulation and reduce weight, making them suitable for load-bearing walls. In contrast, horizontally perforated clay bricks have horizontal cavities, improving moisture control and structural integrity, ideal for non-load-bearing applications. Both types are eco-friendly, energy-efficient building materials, commonly used in construction to promote sustainability and optimize building performance.</p></p>
