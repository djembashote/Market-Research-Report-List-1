<p><p>The Inline Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) market comprises two primary types: desktop and freestanding systems. Desktop AOI systems are compact and designed for use in smaller production environments, offering flexibility and ease of integration with existing workflows. They are ideal for low to moderate-volume inspection tasks. In contrast, freestanding AOI systems are larger, dedicated units suited for high-volume manufacturing lines, providing robust performance and advanced features for thorough inspection of complex assemblies, ensuring higher quality and efficiency in production processes.</p></p>
