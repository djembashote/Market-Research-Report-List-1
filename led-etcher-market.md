<p><ul><li>Dry Etcher</li><li>Wet Etcher</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED etcher market is divided into two primary types: dry etchers and wet etchers. Dry etchers utilize gases in a vacuum to remove materials from the substrate, allowing for precise patterning and minimal chemical waste, making them ideal for advanced semiconductor applications. Wet etchers, on the other hand, employ liquid chemicals to achieve similar results, often favoring simpler processes for less intricate designs. Both types serve critical roles in the production of LED components, addressing various manufacturing needs and efficiencies.</p></p>
