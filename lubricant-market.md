<p><p>The lubricant market comprises three main types: mineral, synthetic, and semi-synthetic lubricants. Mineral lubricants are derived from refined crude oil and are widely used due to their affordability and availability. Synthetic lubricants, engineered from chemical compounds, offer superior performance and durability, making them ideal for high-stress applications. Semi-synthetic lubricants blend mineral and synthetic oils, providing enhanced properties at a lower cost than full synthetics. Each type caters to specific needs, balancing performance, cost, and environmental considerations in various industries.</p></p>
