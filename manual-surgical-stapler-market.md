<p><p>The manual surgical stapler market is categorized into two main types: disposable surgical staplers and reusable surgical staplers. Disposable surgical staplers are designed for single-use, providing sterility and convenience for specific procedures. They reduce the risk of cross-contamination but contribute to medical waste. Both types cater to varying surgical needs, balancing efficiency and safety.</p></p>
