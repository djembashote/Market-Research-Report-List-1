<p><p>The masking tapes market comprises several key types based on adhesive technology. Silicon-based adhesives are prized for their high-temperature resistance, ideal for automotive and electronics applications. Acrylic-based adhesives offer strong adhesion and UV resistance, making them suitable for outdoor use. Rubber-based adhesives provide excellent conformability and quick stick, often used in general-purpose applications. Non-adhesive tapes, while lacking adhesive properties, serve for bundling or surface protection, catering to specialized needs across various industries. Each type addresses specific performance requirements.</p></p>
