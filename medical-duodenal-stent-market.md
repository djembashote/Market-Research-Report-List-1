<p><ul><li>Polymer Duodenal Stent</li><li>Metal Duodenal Stent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical duodenal stent market is primarily categorized into two types: polymer duodenal stents and metal duodenal stents. Polymer duodenal stents are made from biocompatible materials, offering flexibility and reduced risk of tissue erosion but may have a shorter lifespan. In contrast, metal duodenal stents, often created from stainless steel or nitinol, provide greater durability and strength, making them suitable for long-term use. The choice between these stent types depends on patient-specific needs and the clinical scenario.</p></p>
