<p><p>The Multi-beam Echo Sounder (MBES) is an advanced sonar technology used primarily in underwater mapping and surveying. <p><p>The multi-beam echo sounder market can be segmented into two primary types: hull installation and portable systems. Hull installation echo sounders are permanently mounted on vessels, providing continuous and high-resolution seabed mapping for commercial and research applications. In contrast, portable systems offer versatility and ease of transport, making them suitable for smaller vessels or temporary surveying tasks. Both types cater to various end-users, including marine research, navigation, and underwater construction, enhancing underwater exploration and data collection capabilities.</p></p>
