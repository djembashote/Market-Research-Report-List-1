<p><strong>The Nano-silica Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>P-type</li><li>S-Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nano-silica market is segmented into two main types: P-type and S-type. P-type nano-silica, or precipitated silica, is primarily used in applications such as tires and coatings due to its high surface area and reinforcement properties. S-type, or fumed silica, is produced through the combustion of silicon tetrachloride and is valued for its anti-caking and thickening properties, making it ideal for use in adhesives, sealants, and various industrial applications. Both types cater to distinct industry needs and applications.</p></p>
