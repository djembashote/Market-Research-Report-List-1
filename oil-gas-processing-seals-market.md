<p><p>In the oil and gas processing seals market, two primary types are prevalent: single seals and double seals. Single seals consist of one sealing element, ideal for lower pressure and temperature applications, providing simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, double seals feature two independent sealing elements, creating a barrier to enhance reliability and prevent leakage in more demanding conditions. This design is crucial for maintaining safety and protecting the environment, making double seals suitable for high-risk scenarios in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
