<p><ul><li>Image Intensifier</li><li>Thermal Image</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical sight market features two primary technologies: image intensifiers and thermal imaging. Image intensifiers enhance low-light vision by amplifying available light, allowing users to see in darkness, making them ideal for nighttime operations. Thermal imaging, on the other hand, detects heat signatures, providing visibility in complete darkness, fog, or smoke. Both technologies serve crucial roles in military, law enforcement, and outdoor activities, offering distinct advantages for target acquisition, surveillance, and situational awareness regardless of environmental conditions.</p></p>
