<p><ul><li>Aligner</li><li>Wire</li><li>Molar Bands</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The orthodontics treatment product market comprises three primary types: aligners, wires, and molar bands. Aligners are transparent, removable trays that gradually shift teeth into the desired position, offering aesthetic appeal. Wires are used with brackets to create tension that guides teeth alignment, playing a crucial role in traditional braces. Molar bands are metal rings cemented to the back teeth, providing anchor points for wires and other orthodontic appliances. Each product serves a specific function in orthodontic treatment, catering to various patient needs.</p></p>
