<p><p>The plastic pigment market is primarily categorized into two types: inorganic and organic pigments. Inorganic pigments, such as titanium dioxide and chromium oxide, are known for their durability, heat resistance, and excellent opacity, making them suitable for industrial applications. Conversely, organic pigments are derived from carbon-based materials, offering vibrant colors and better transparency, often used in consumer products like toys and packaging. Both types serve essential roles, influencing color performance, stability, and application versatility in plastics.</p></p>
