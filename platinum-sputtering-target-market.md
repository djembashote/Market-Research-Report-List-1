<p><p>The platinum sputtering target market is categorized into two main types: rotary and non-rotatable types. Rotary sputtering targets are designed to facilitate consistent and uniform deposition by rotating during the sputtering process, enhancing target utilization and reducing material waste. Non-rotatable targets, on the other hand, are fixed in place and serve specific applications where motion is not required. Both types are essential in various industries, including electronics and coatings, depending on deposition needs and production efficiency.</p></p>
