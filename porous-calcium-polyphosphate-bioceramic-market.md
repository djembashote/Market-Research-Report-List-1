<p><p>The Porous Calcium Polyphosphate Bioceramic market is divided into two main types: biological inert ceramics and bioactive ceramics. Biological inert ceramics are designed to be non-reactive and provide structural support without interacting with biological tissues. In contrast, bioactive ceramics actively engage with surrounding biological environments, promoting tissue integration and healing through chemical bonding with bone. Both types serve critical roles in applications like bone repair and dental implants, catering to diverse biomedical needs.</p></p>
