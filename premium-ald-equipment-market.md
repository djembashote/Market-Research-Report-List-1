<p><strong>The Premium ALD Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PEALD</li><li>Thermal ALD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Premium ALD Equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: Plasma-Enhanced Atomic Layer Deposition (PEALD) and Thermal Atomic Layer Deposition (Thermal ALD). PEALD utilizes plasma to enhance chemical reactions at lower temperatures, enabling the deposition of thin films on a variety of substrates, including heat-sensitive materials. In contrast, Thermal ALD relies on the sequential exposure of a substrate to precursors, resulting in uniform film growth at higher temperatures. Both technologies are crucial for advanced semiconductor and nanotechnology applications.</p></p>
