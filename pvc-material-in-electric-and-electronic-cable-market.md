Two primary methods for producing PVC are the Calcium Carbide Method, which involves the reaction of calcium carbide with acetylene to produce vinyl chloride, and the Ethylene Method, which uses ethylene to create vinyl chloride monomer. Each method influences the quality, cost, and environmental impact of the final PVC products used in various cable applications.</p></p>
