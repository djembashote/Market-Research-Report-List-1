<p><ul><li>Volume Coils</li><li>Surface Coils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiofrequency coil market comprises various types, including volume coils and surface coils. Volume coils are designed to create a uniform magnetic field and capture signals from larger anatomical regions, making them suitable for whole-body imaging. In contrast, surface coils are smaller and optimized for high-resolution imaging of specific areas, providing enhanced signal-to-noise ratios. Each type serves distinct applications in magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), accommodating different clinical needs and improving diagnostic accuracy in medical imaging.</p></p>
