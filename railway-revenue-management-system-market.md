<p><p>Railway Revenue Management Systems (RMS) can be categorized into two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. </p><p>On-premise RMS involves software installed locally on the railway company's hardware, offering greater control over data and systems but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. </p><p>In contrast, cloud-based RMS provides a hosted solution accessible via the internet, allowing for scalability, reduced operational costs, and easier updates.
