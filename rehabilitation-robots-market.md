<p><p>The rehabilitation robots market can be categorized into three main types: lower limb, exoskeleton, and upper limb robots. Lower limb robots focus on aiding rehabilitation of the legs, helping patients recover mobility after injuries or surgeries. Exoskeletons provide external support and enhance movement for individuals with mobility impairments, often used in both rehabilitation and assistive contexts. Upper limb robots assist in rehabilitation of the arms and hands, promoting strength and coordination recovery for patients with upper extremity injuries or conditions.</p></p>
