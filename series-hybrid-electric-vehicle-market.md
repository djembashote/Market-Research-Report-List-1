<p><strong>The Series Hybrid Electric Vehicle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Parallel"</li><li>"Series"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Series Hybrid Electric Vehicle (HEV) market is categorized into two main types: parallel and series configurations. In a parallel hybrid, both the internal combustion engine and electric motor can drive the vehicle simultaneously or separately, enhancing efficiency and performance. In contrast, a series hybrid relies solely on the electric motor for propulsion, with the engine functioning only as a generator to recharge the battery. This results in optimized energy usage and can lead to lower emissions and improved fuel efficiency.</p></p>
