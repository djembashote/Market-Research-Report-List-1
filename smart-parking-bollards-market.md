<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Powered</li><li>Pneumatic Powered</li><li>Electric Powered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart parking bollards are advanced solutions for managing parking spaces, categorized into three main types: hydraulic powered, pneumatic powered, and electric powered. </p><p>Hydraulic powered bollards utilize fluid pressure to raise and lower the bollard, offering robust security and durability. Pneumatic powered bollards use compressed air for operation, providing rapid deployment and retraction. Electric powered bollards rely on motors for movement, allowing for easy integration with smart systems. Each type enhances parking management through automation, security, and space optimization.</p></p>
