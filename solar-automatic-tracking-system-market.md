<p><p>The Solar Automatic Tracking System market encompasses two main types: Single Axis and Dual Axis systems. Single Axis systems track the sun's movement along one axis, optimizing sunlight capture throughout the day, which enhances energy output and efficiency. In contrast, Dual Axis systems adjust both horizontally and vertically, following the sun's path more precisely through seasonal changes. This greater level of tracking maximizes solar exposure and energy production, making both types valuable for improving the efficiency of solar energy systems.</p></p>
