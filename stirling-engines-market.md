<p><p>The Stirling engine market is classified into three main types: Alpha, Beta, and Gamma Stirling engines. Alpha Stirling engines feature two distinct cylinders for the hot and cold sides, offering high efficiency and simplicity. Beta Stirling engines use a single cylinder with a displacer and power piston, suitable for smaller applications. Gamma Stirling engines also have separate cylinders but include a displacer within a single power piston, making them versatile for both stationary and mobile applications. Each type caters to specific industry needs.</p></p>
