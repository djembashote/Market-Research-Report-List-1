<p><strong>The Stud Welding Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitor Discharge (CD) Stud Welding</li><li>Arc Stud Welding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stud welding machines market comprises two primary types: Capacitor Discharge (CD) stud welding and Arc stud welding. CD stud welding uses stored electrical energy to weld studs quickly to thin materials, making it ideal for applications requiring minimal distortion and quick cycles. In contrast, Arc stud welding relies on an electric arc to melt the stud and base material, providing deep penetration and strong bonds, suited for thicker materials and structural applications. Both methods serve distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
