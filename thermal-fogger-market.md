<p><p>The thermal fogger market comprises three main types: gas foggers, fuel foggers, and electric foggers. Gas foggers utilize propane or butane as fuel, producing a dense fog ideal for large outdoor applications. Fuel foggers, typically powered by gasoline, offer high mobility and extended operation, suitable for agricultural and pest control use. Electric foggers, powered by electricity, are cleaner and quieter, making them ideal for indoor applications and areas requiring precision, such as residential settings and small gardens.</p></p>
