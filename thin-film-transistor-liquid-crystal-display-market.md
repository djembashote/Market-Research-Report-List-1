<p><p>The Thin-Film Transistor-Liquid Crystal Display (TFT-LCD) market is primarily segmented into three types: Twisted Nematic (TN), Vertical Alignment (VA), and In-Plane Switching (IPS). TN displays are cost-effective with fast response times, making them popular in budget devices but lack wide viewing angles and color accuracy. VA panels offer better contrast and deeper blacks, suitable for home entertainment. IPS technology provides superior color reproduction and wider viewing angles, making it ideal for professional applications and high-end devices. Each type caters to specific user needs.</p></p>
