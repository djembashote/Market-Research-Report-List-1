<p><p>Total Ossicular Replacement Prosthesis (TORP) is a medical device used in otology to replace one or more components of the middle ear's ossicular chain that are damaged or missing due to conditions such as chronic otitis media or trauma. The prosthesis enables the transmission of sound vibrations from the eardrum to the inner ear, thereby restoring hearing functionality. </p><p>The TORP market is witnessing significant growth driven by an increasing prevalence of ear disorders, rising geriatric populations, and advancements in surgical techniques and materials.
