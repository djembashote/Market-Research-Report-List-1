<p><strong>The Transdermal Gel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ionophoresis</li><li>Electroporation</li><li>Sonophoresis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transdermal gel market encompasses various technologies for enhancing drug delivery through the skin. Ionophoresis uses electrical currents to drive charged drug molecules into the dermis. Electroporation leverages short electrical pulses to create temporary pores in the skin, facilitating larger molecules' absorption. Sonophoresis employs ultrasound waves to increase skin permeability, enabling deeper penetration of therapeutic agents. Together, these methods enhance the efficacy and speed of drug delivery in pharmaceutical applications, catering to diverse medical needs.</p></p>
