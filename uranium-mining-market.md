<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Uranium Mining Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Granite-Type Uranium Deposits</li><li>Volcanic-Type Uranium Deposits</li><li>Sandstone-Type Uranium Deposits</li><li>Carbonate-Siliceous-Pelitic Rock Type Uranium Deposits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The uranium mining market features several deposit types which influence extraction methods and market dynamics. Granite-type deposits are found in crystalline rocks and typically require underground mining. Volcanic-type deposits result from volcanic activity and can be mined using both open-pit and underground methods. Sandstone-type deposits are sedimentary, often amenable to in-situ recovery, making them cost-effective. Lastly, carbonate-siliceous-pelitic rock type deposits, associated with sedimentary basins, vary in accessibility but are significant for their uranium yields in specific geological settings.</p></p>
