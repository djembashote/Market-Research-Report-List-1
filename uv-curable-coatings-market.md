<p><strong>The UV-Curable Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV Base-coat</li><li>UV Mid-coat</li><li>UV Top-coat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV-curable coatings market is segmented into three main types: UV base-coat, UV mid-coat, and UV top-coat. UV base-coats provide adhesion and initial protection on substrates, forming a hard layer. UV mid-coats enhance appearance and contribute depth, serving as an intermediary layer for aesthetics. UV top-coats offer the final protective finish, resisting abrasion, chemicals, and UV light, ensuring durability and a high-quality surface. Together, these coatings deliver comprehensive solutions for various applications in industries like automotive, furniture, and electronics.</p></p>
