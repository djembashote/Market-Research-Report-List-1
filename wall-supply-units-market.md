<p><p>Wall Supply Units (WSUs) are categorized into two main market types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal WSUs are designed to be installed along walls and offer a more stretched design, often used in larger spaces to distribute air efficiently. Both types serve the same purpose of enhancing air distribution and climate control but cater to different spatial requirements and aesthetic preferences.</p></p>
