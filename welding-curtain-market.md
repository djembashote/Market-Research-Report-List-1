<p><p>A welding curtain is a protective barrier made from flame-resistant materials, such as PVC or vinyl, designed to shield workers and surrounding areas from sparks, UV radiation, and debris generated during welding processes. These curtains are essential for ensuring workplace safety, preventing accidents, and maintaining compliance with occupational safety regulations. They are widely used in industries like construction, automotive, manufacturing, and shipbuilding.</p><p>The Welding Curtain Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.8% during the forecast period.
