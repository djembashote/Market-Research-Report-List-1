<p><p>The windscreen cleaner market caters to different glass types, including laminated and tempered glass. Laminated glass, used in vehicle windshields, consists of layers bonded for safety and durability. Tempered glass, found in side and rear windows, is heat-treated for strength and shatters into small pieces. Cleaners for these surfaces are formulated to avoid damage and ensure clarity. Dry-wet mixing cleaners combine dry and liquid components for effective dirt removal, suitable for both glass types, offering convenience and efficiency in cleaning applications.</p></p>
