<p><strong>The Wireless Holter Monitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>1-channel</li><li>2-channel</li><li>3-channel</li><li>7-channel</li><li>12-channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wireless Holter monitors come in various channel configurations that determine the number of leads used to record the heart's electrical activity. The 1-channel monitor records data from a single lead, primarily used for basic assessments. 2-channel monitors provide dual-lead recordings for more comprehensive analysis. 3-channel and 7-channel monitors offer increased detail for diagnosing arrhythmias or other heart conditions. The 12-channel monitors deliver the most extensive insights, suitable for complex cardiac evaluations, catering to diverse clinical needs in cardiology.</p></p>
